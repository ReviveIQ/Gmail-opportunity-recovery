Label System

Apply these labels to flag emails worth acting on. Everything else gets archived or deleted.

Opportunity/Revenue Opportunity/Career Opportunity/Network Opportunity/ReviveIQ Action/Follow-Up Assets/Reference

When to apply each

Revenue — B2B leads, partnership offers, consulting inquiries, client follow-ups
Career — recruiter outreach, hiring manager contacts, referrals, job leads
Network — people worth reconnecting with, introductions, relationship maintenance
ReviveIQ — prospects, advisory contacts, potential B2B clients
Daily Process

Daily Triage

Every email gets one of four actions. Nothing sits untouched.

1
Delete
No value, no history, no relationship. Gone.
2
Archive
Might need it someday. Out of inbox, still searchable.
3
Label as Opportunity
Apply the relevant label. This email has future value.
4
Create Follow-Up
Action required. Label Action/Follow-Up and set a reminder.
Decision tree — does this email have opportunity value?

Ask: can this generate any of the following?

💰 Revenue
💼 Employment
🤝 Partnership
📈 Relationship Capital
YES →
Apply opportunity label. Add to weekly review queue.
NO →
Archive or delete. Remove from inbox immediately.
